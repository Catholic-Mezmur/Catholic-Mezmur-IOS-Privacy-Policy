1. Personal Information Collection and Usage

Profile Creation: When you create a profile, we collect your name, email address, and any other information you provide.

Song Uploads: When you upload songs or hymns, we store these files along with any metadata or descriptions you provide.

Usage Data: We collect data about how you use our app, such as the songs you view and the features you use. This data helps us improve app functionality and user experience.

2. Uploaded Content Verification

Verification Process: All uploaded songs or hymns are reviewed by administrators for appropriate language, formatting, and relevance.

Posting & Rejection: Approved content is posted; rejected content is not, and users receive notification with opportunity to revise.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Storage and Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Access, Correction, and Deletion

You can access, correct, or delete your personal information through the app or by contacting us.

6. External Data Transmission

Our app uploads images to fonts.gstatic.com for processing. All data is secured and deleted post-processing. By using the app you consent to this.

7. User Consent & App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

We do not track users across other apps or websites. If this changes, we will request explicit permission per Apple’s ATT guidelines.

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described, unless longer retention is required by law.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13; if discovered, we delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy
